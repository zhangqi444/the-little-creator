## Our Story

The Little Maker started as one family's journey into robot programming. We quickly realized that the hardest part isn't the robots — it's finding good information, supportive teammates, and a community that gets it. So we built one.

We compete in FLL and VEX, and we document everything along the way so the next family doesn't have to start from scratch.
