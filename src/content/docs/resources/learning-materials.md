## Programming & Robotics Fundamentals

### For Younger Kids (Ages 8–12)
- **[Code.org](https://code.org)** — Free, browser-based intro to coding with fun themes
- **Scratch (MIT)** — Block-based programming that builds the same logic used in FLL
- **LEGO Education lessons** — Official lesson plans included with SPIKE Prime kits

### For Older Kids (Ages 12+)
- **[CS50 (Harvard, free)](https://cs50.harvard.edu)** — Excellent intro to computer science
- **[Python.org Beginner Guide](https://wiki.python.org/moin/BeginnersGuide)** — Python official tutorials
- **VEX Robotics curriculum** — Available free on the VEX Education portal

## Books We Like

- *Invent Your Own Computer Games with Python* — Al Sweigart (free online)
- *Python Crash Course* — Eric Matthes (great for self-teaching kids)
- *The Art of Problem Solving* series — For math-minded kids heading toward STEM competitions

## YouTube Channels

- **FIRST Lego League (official)** — Season reveals, tips, team spotlights
- **VEX Robotics (official)** — Game reveals, build tutorials
- **Tutorials with your specific kit** — Search "[SPIKE Prime tutorial]" or "[VEX IQ tutorial]" for community walkthroughs

## FLL-Specific

- Game manuals are the most important document each season — read them carefully
- **FLL Tutorials YouTube playlists** — Many coaches share their walkthroughs publicly
- FLL Coach community on Facebook groups — surprisingly active and helpful
