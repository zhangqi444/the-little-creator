This page maps FLL regions to their Program Delivery Organizations (PDOs) and documents what we could verify directly from each PDO's website. Where we could not verify a specific detail (fee, exact open date), we say so and direct you to the source.

**How to use this page:** Find your region below. Use the verified registration URL. For anything marked "confirm on their site," go check — PDO pages update seasonally and we can't guarantee a detail is still current.

:::tip[Two steps always required]
All U.S. teams need: (1) national registration at [my.firstinspires.org](https://my.firstinspires.org), then (2) regional registration with your PDO. Both are required. National registration opens in spring; regional registration timing varies.
:::

## United States

### Washington

- **PDO:** FIRST Washington
- **FLL page:** https://firstwa.org/fll-challenge/
- **Registration page:** https://firstwa.org/challenge-registration/
- **Events:** https://firstwa.org/events/
- **Verified:** Site is live, FLL Challenge page confirmed, registration page confirmed as of May 2026
- **What to confirm on their site:** Current season's registration open date and local fee

### Oregon

- **PDO:** ORTOP (Oregon Robotics Tournament & Outreach Program)
- **FLL page:** https://ortop.org/fll
- **Registration page:** https://ortop.org/for-teams/season-registration/
- **Contact:** registrar@ortop.org | 503-486-7622
- **Verified from their site (May 2026):**
  - All Oregon teams must register with both FIRST (national) and ORTOP each season
  - FIRST national registration opened in May (2026)
  - ORTOP (Oregon) registration is open now for FLL Challenge, Explore, and FTC
  - Register with FIRST first, then ORTOP — ORTOP registration requires your FIRST team number
  - Financial assistance available through ORTOP for teams that need it
- **What to confirm:** Current ORTOP fee (listed on their registration page, varies by program)

### Texas

- **PDO:** FIRST in Texas
- **FLL page:** https://firstintx.com/programs/fll-challenge/
- **Registration page:** https://firstintx.com/programs/fll-challenge/ (registration links on this page)
- **Events:** https://firstintx.com/events/
- **Grants:** https://firstintx.com/team-grants/
- **Verified:** FLL Challenge page live, grants page live, as of prior research
- **What to confirm:** Current season's local fee and registration open date

### California — Southern (SoCal)

- **PDO:** FIRST Robotics California (CA FIRST)
- **FLL page:** https://cafirst.org/fll/ (redirects to https://cafirst.org/fll-ca-southern/)
- **Verified:** FLL page live, covers FLL Challenge for Southern California region; hosts Southern California Championship and Central California Championship; also hosted Western Edge Open
- **Important:** California is split — Southern CA uses CA FIRST; Northern CA uses FIRST NorCal (firstnorcal.org). If you are unsure which covers your zip code, use [FIRST Find Local Support](https://www.firstinspires.org/find-local-support)
- **What to confirm:** Registration portal link (not prominently surfaced on their FLL page — contact them directly or use FIRST Dashboard)

### California — Northern (NorCal)

- **PDO:** FIRST NorCal
- **Homepage:** https://firstnorcal.org
- **Verified:** Domain live (May 2026), FLL-specific subpage not reachable via direct URL — navigate from homepage
- **What to confirm:** Their FLL page URL and registration process; navigate from homepage or use [FIRST Find Local Support](https://www.firstinspires.org/find-local-support)

### Florida

- **PDO:** FIRST in Florida
- **FLL page:** https://firstinflorida.org/programs/first-lego-league
- **Verified:** Page live (May 2026), covers FLL Challenge, Explore, Discover; described as "largest K-12 robotics program in Florida"
- **What to confirm:** Registration open date and local fee; registration links are on the FLL page

### Michigan

- **PDO:** FIRST in Michigan
- **FLL Challenge page:** https://firstinmichigan.us/FLL-Challenge
- **Verified:** Page live (May 2026), covers FLL Challenge
- **What to confirm:** Registration open date, local fee, and specific registration portal link — check the FLL Challenge page directly

### Mid-Atlantic (New Jersey, Pennsylvania, Delaware)

- **PDO:** FIRST Mid-Atlantic (FMA)
- **FLL page:** https://midatlanticrobotics.com/fll/
- **Verified:** Page live (May 2026), covers NJ/PA/DE; page title "FMA-FLL Home"
- **Note:** FIRST Pennsylvania (firstpa.org) also operates in PA — if unsure which covers your area, use [FIRST Find Local Support](https://www.firstinspires.org/find-local-support)
- **What to confirm:** Registration open date, local fee, specific registration link — on their FLL page

### Minnesota, North Dakota, South Dakota

- **PDO:** FIRST in Upper Midwest
- **Homepage:** https://firstuppermidwest.org (formerly mnfirst.org — redirects)
- **Verified:** Site live (May 2026); covers MN, ND, SD; primarily FRC-focused on homepage
- **What to confirm:** Navigate to their Programs section for FLL-specific pages and registration; or use [FIRST Find Local Support](https://www.firstinspires.org/find-local-support) to confirm the right contact

### States not listed above

For states not listed here, use the official **FIRST Find Local Support** tool:

- **URL:** https://www.firstinspires.org/find-local-support
- Enter your zip code or state to find your Program Delivery Organization
- This is the authoritative directory — always more current than this page

## International

### Germany, Austria, Switzerland (DACH)

- **PDO:** HANDS on TECHNOLOGY e.V.
- **Site:** https://www.first-lego-league.org
- **Verified (from DACH site):** Registration opens late April / early May; runs until end of October subject to capacity. Classes can register year-round. Fees in EUR. Bilingual (German/English)
- **Important:** DACH registration dates and fees apply **only** to Germany, Austria, and Switzerland. Do not use DACH dates for U.S. planning.

### United Kingdom

- **PDO:** IET (Institution of Engineering and Technology)
- **Site:** https://fll.ieeefoundation.org (IET runs FLL in the UK)
- **Verified:** UK is transitioning to Future Edition — see [IET FLL Hub](https://education.theiet.org/first-lego-league/) for current UK program info
- **What to confirm:** UK registration timing from IET directly

### Australia

- **PDO:** FIRST Australia
- **FLL page:** https://firstaustralia.org/first-lego-league/
- **Verified:** FIRST Australia is the national PDO; hosts APOC (Asia-Pacific Open Championship) for top regional teams; 2026-27 BIOGLOW season announced
- **What to confirm:** State-level registration (Australia operates through state affiliates) — start at firstaustralia.org

### Canada

- **PDO:** FIRST Robotics Canada (national); provincial affiliates for local events
- **Site:** https://firstroboticscanada.org
- **Note:** Site is behind Cloudflare verification — visit directly for current info
- **What to confirm:** Your provincial affiliate (Ontario, BC, Alberta, Quebec, etc.) for local registration dates and fees; national registration still through FIRST Dashboard
