Looking for teammates? Have open spots on your team? This is the place.

## How to Post

Open a GitHub Discussion in the **Find a Team** category and include:

- Your general location (city/region is enough)
- Competition you're targeting (FLL, VEX IQ, VEX V5)
- Kids' ages and experience level
- What you're looking for (teammates, a coach, a mentor, etc.)
- How to reach you

## Tips for Finding Teammates

- **Local schools and libraries** often host robotics clubs — check with your school STEM coordinator
- **Tournament spectating** — attending a competition before you compete is a great way to meet families and see what teams look like up close
- **FIRST's team finder** — [ftc-events.firstinspires.org](https://ftc-events.firstinspires.org) has a team locator for official FIRST programs
- **VEX's RobotEvents** — [robotevents.com](https://www.robotevents.com) lists registered teams by region
